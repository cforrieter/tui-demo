Perfect family holiday

26 Feb , 2016

We were optimistic about the location of the hotel due to me and my partner being in our early 20's but we could not have asked for a more relaxing and peaceful holiday! Food - I'm starting with the food because it really is SO GOOD. On arrival (around 11:00 in the evening) we were pleased to find that the buffet restraint had been kept open, the food was fresh and the chef was still bringing out fresh food even at such a late time!. The food on offer was always greatly varied which when your staying all inclusive is a great change to the usual repetitive dishes. The chefs were fantastic and I 100% recommend the gala night where the chefs and other member of staff get recognition for their hard work, The layout - As you have probably read the hotel is big...in fact I would even go on to say it is huge. It is on a very steep slope and the rooms and other amenities are rather spaced out. Our room was located near the entrance of the hotel so getting back to the room after being at the pool (located at the top) was easy however getting up was sometimes a struggle in the heat. The hotel has a fantastic buggy cart/ car services where you call to reception and within minutes a member of the team will drive you to the top. There is also the funicular. I had read very mixed reviews on this but honestly, it was no issue what so ever. Yes it is slow but the views are so beautiful this gives you an opportunity to take it all in. There is air con inside and seats, although we sometimes had to stand again this just allowed us to really appreciate the views. All in all for a big hotel the staff cater well for it, we managed to get around fine however if you do not fancy the possibility of walking, I would perhaps see what rooms they have on offer closer to the main amenities. The room - We could not have been happier with our room. The rooms are huge, clean and comfortable, which ticks all my boxes! The balconies are large with a table and 2 chairs and we had a beautiful view across the sea and the hills, which was absolutely stunning to wake up to in the morning. The bathroom was spotless and the cleanliness was maintained during our entire stay. A lovely powerful shower and a large bath meant that even back in our rooms we could continue relaxing. Pool area- The pool are was one of the things that attracted me to the hotel. The pool are is high up and gives a beautiful view across the sea. It is large enough for a swim and was perfectly located to have areas of shade and areas of sunshine. The loungers were comfortable and we never struggled to get 4 beds together. There were a substantial amount of parasols and tables which all showed no damage. The waitresses are prompt in coming around and taking your drinks order, even though there is a bar just a stones throw away this was a nice touch on our lazier days! The staff- The best staff I have came across in a hotel! Always happy to help, always smiling and always wanting the best for guests! I honestly cannot fault the staff from the chefs, the reception team, the waiting / bar staff to the reps, all were fantastic! One little problem was the wasps! The hotel have tried to implement ways of getting rid of the pesky bugs but they were relentless. We were unable to eat outside and wasted a number of drinks due to them sneaking in to the glasses! All in all, as I am sure you can guess by this glowing review, I really cannot thank the hotel enough for such a lovely stay and hope to visit again soon!More
