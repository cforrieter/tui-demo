10 Great Things About Our Adult Holidays

   
  • Adults Only
    Whether you’re after a full-on adults-only hotel or a place with adult-only zones – stylish décor and peaceful pool scenes are the order of the day.
  • Home-from-home Holidays
    Afternoon tea, Sunday roasts and classic entertainment – that’s to be expected on a Gold holiday.
  • Bars and Restaurants
    Even the most family-friendly hotels usually have a spot that’s just for adults – expect piano bars, cocktail lounges and candlelit restaurants.
  • Adult-only Areas
    Adult-only zones allow you to relax in the peace and quiet, and usually come with dedicated pools.
  • Locations Near Nightlife
    A fairy-lit marina edged by chic wine bars, or a neon-lit strip lined with nightclubs? Whichever you prefer, choose a property in the heart of the action.
  • Luxe spas
    Neck, back and shoulder massage? Chocolate body scrub? Lots of our hotels offer state-of-the-art spas with lengthy treatment menus.
  • Evening Entertainment
    Live shows, quizzes and comedy acts all feature on evening line-ups. In our Sensatori resorts, we’ll even fly UK acts over to entertain you.
  • Adult-focused Excursions
    Choose from a variety of tours tailored to adults, like wine tastings, cooking lessons and scenic walking tours.
  • Peaceful Boltholes
    We’ve sourced the best off-the-beaten-track hotels – from family-run places in Italy’s coastal villages, to tropical island hideaways in Mauritius.
  • Active Holidays
    It's not all about taking it easy - we've got hotels with diving centres on the doorstep and properties in prime locations for golfing.

Adult-Only Holidays

Our adult holidays run the full scale – you can choose from off-the-beaten-track hideaways, like those in our Small & Friendly collection, or opt to be right in the thick of things, à la Thomson Scene hotels.

Location isn’t the only thing you’ve got to think about on an adult holiday. If you fancy a totally child-free environment, consider a Thomson Couples hotel, which will become part of the Thomson Sensimar collection from May 2016. These are adults-only, so a peaceful atmosphere is guaranteed. His and hers spa treatments and out-of-this-world dining come courtesy of our luxurious Sensatori resorts. And if you feel like getting all dressed up for dinner and a show, look to our classic Thomson Gold holidays.
